About Us

We are a group of students with one goal: building a greater, more collaborative computing community at Bangladesh Agricultural University.

Activities

CodeLab

At Codelab, we provide expert mentorship in both basic and data structure programming to help you establish a solid coding foundation. Our experienced mentors guide you through core programming concepts, from basic syntax to advanced data structures such as arrays, linked lists, and trees. Through hands-on projects and personalized support, you'll acquire practical experience and problem-solving skills essential for excelling in software development. Whether you're just starting or aiming to enhance your skills, Codelab is dedicated to supporting your growth and helping you become a proficient and confident programmer.

Weekly Workshop

Our weekly workshop focuses on key algorithms such as dynamic programming and greedy techniques. Participants gain hands-on experience and practical insights, enhancing their problem-solving skills and coding proficiency.
